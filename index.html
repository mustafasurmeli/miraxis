Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how MIRAXIS (package: com.surmeli.miraxis) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our Android application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information automatically when you use the app:

  • Device identifiers — including your Advertising ID assigned by Android.
  • Gameplay data — scores, session duration, level completion status, and in-game interactions.
  • Crash reports and technical diagnostics — stack traces, device model, OS version, and app version at the time of a crash or error.
  • Language and region settings — your device language preference and general region, used to serve content in one of our 15 supported languages.

2. How We Use Information

  • Improve game experience and fix bugs — We use Firebase Analytics to understand how players interact with the game, and Firebase Crashlytics to identify and resolve technical issues.
  • Display relevant advertisements — We use Google AdMob to show banner, interstitial, rewarded, and native ads. AdMob may use your Advertising ID to serve personalized or non-personalized ads based on your preferences.
  • We do NOT sell personal data to third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase Analytics
    https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics
    https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
  • Google AdMob
    https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Data Transfer

The data collected through our app may be transferred to and processed on Google servers located in the United States and other countries. Google complies with applicable data protection frameworks, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

5. Data Retention

  • Analytics data: 14 months (Firebase default retention period).
  • Crash reports: 90 days.

6. Your Rights

  • Opt out of personalized ads: On your Android device, go to Settings → Privacy → Ads and enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization" or delete your advertising ID.
  • Request data deletion: You may contact us at the email address below to request deletion of any data associated with your device.

7. Children's Privacy

MIRAXIS does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information as soon as possible.
